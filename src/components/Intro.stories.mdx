# Ridecell design system and component library
***

### Principles and Goals
* Standardize interaction design patterns and design styles across Ridecell products
* Easily reuse and share documentation / code
* Allow contribution by multiple parties in the future in order to grow the library
* A tool to drive design strategy 
* Helps designers and engineers facilitate design conversations
* Improves collaboration between Design, Engineering, and Product

You can also check the [implementation of the lirbary](https://github.com/Ridecell/web-components-lib).
